If you have ever read a blog post on Medium.com, you will see that how much readable the post design is. The line width is enough so that you don't have to move your face a lot, the font size, line height and margins etc are good and readable.

No matter on which device you are reading, it is easy enough for you to read because the desgin is so good. That is what i tried to replicate on my blog for today.
